Texas-rooted financial education

Financial literacy for everyone.

LearnTheMoney provides free, community-based financial literacy programs because access to money knowledge should never depend on income, background, or circumstance.

Built through real-world education efforts across Texas and Nueces County, LearnTheMoney brings together practical workshops, program resources, and public-impact learning opportunities designed to make financial education more accessible and more human.
